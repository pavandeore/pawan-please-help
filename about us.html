ABOUT NUTTY

NUTY is India’s first company to launch extended shelf-life, ready-to-eat curries, and meals using non-thermal cold pasteurization through its proprietary cooking process aided by HPP (High-Pressure Processing) technology. NUTY is also India’s first omnichannel Food-As-A-Service company using modern food technologies, processes and logistics to offer gratifying, healthy meal options affordably. Thus focused on ready-to-eat meals that are affordable, nutritious, tasty, hygienic food with a long shelf-life From Authentic Indian Curries, Biryani’s, Khichdi’s to Arabic, Italian, Japanese, Mexican, Pan Asian, Spanish and Thai Cuisines.
