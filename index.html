Who we are

We are ‘The Food Dost’ Distribution Channel Partner’s of NUTY. A brief background on us. Zameer Shroff (Partner, The Food Dost): Zameer comes from affluent Gujarati Family, taking care of family business; 'Taruni Shroff Marriage Bureau, Mumbai' which is one of the oldest professional marriage bureau working for Gujarati clients all across the globe. Zameer's passion for food led him to the food business and is the founding partner. Vaibhav Shende (Partner, The Food Dost): I hail from a family with 30 plus years Bharatgas Distrbutorship along with Elica and Vaya of recent. With 10 years of Construction and Interior Design experience in United States, when I came back to India felt the need to diversify and enter the food industry due to the slowdown of construction and realty. With passion for food and global connections of Zameer and with Vaibhav’s experience in distribution and Project Management we came together for 'The Food Dost'. Food is among the most basic of human needs, and most often working urban millennial’s have to make compromises of taste, health or money every day due to non-availability of any existing options. We deliver an option that delivers with no compromise to any of the above factors
